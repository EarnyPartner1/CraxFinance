Building the Future of Finance

We're on a mission to democratize financial intelligence and empower everyone to make smarter investment decisions through AI-powered technology.

Our Mission

At Finova, we believe that everyone deserves access to sophisticated financial tools and insights, regardless of their background or net worth. Our mission is to level the playing field by bringing institutional-grade financial intelligence to individual investors.

Through cutting-edge AI technology and a user-centric approach, we're transforming how people interact with their finances, making complex investment strategies accessible and actionable for everyone.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to transforming the financial industry

2020

Foundation

Finova was founded by Jennifer Smith with a vision to democratize financial intelligence using AI technology.

2021

Seed Funding

Raised $5M in seed funding from leading venture capital firms to build our initial product and team.

2022

Product Launch

Launched our first AI-powered investment platform with portfolio analytics and personalized recommendations.

2023

Series A & Growth

Secured $25M in Series A funding and expanded our user base to over 100,000 active investors.

2024

Global Expansion

Expanded our services to 15+ countries and introduced advanced features like automated trading and tax optimization.
